Troxler's Fading

How to use this experiment
  1. Enter your name in the Participant field on the right panel.
  2. Adjust the Cross Size and Spot Size using the +/− buttons or arrow keys.
  3. Press Start Timer (or Space) and fix your gaze on the central cross. Do not move your eyes.
  4. When the spots appear to fade, press Record Vanish (or T). Your time is saved automatically.
  5. Press Reset (or R) to run another trial. Repeat as needed.
Tip: Use keyboard shortcuts for a hands-free experience — Space to start, T to record, R to reset, and Arrow keys to adjust sizes.
